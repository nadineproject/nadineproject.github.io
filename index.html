This is Nadine.

She helps people run
coworking spaces.

An open source coworking platform. Raised by Office Nomads since 2007.

nadine grew up to help with:

Communications:

member profiles, discovery, discussion lists, interest tags.

Reporting:

tracking activity, analytics, billing reports.

Work Flow:

sign-in, sign-up, onboarding and exiting tasks.

Now Office Nomads wants to share Nadine with other coworking spaces around the world!
